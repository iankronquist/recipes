    Gruyere Quiche with Asparagus and Onion

    Total time: 90 minutes

    From: Our Kitchen

    Ingredients

    • 1 tbsp butter
    • 4 eggs
    • Nutmeg
    • 1 chilled pie crust (or homemade. Whatever floats your boat)
    • 1 onion, chopped
    • 1 bunch asparagus, chopped
    • 1 1/4 cups half and half
    • 1 cup gruyere cheese (shredded)


    Directions

    1. Preheat oven to 350* F
    2. Saute onion and asparagus in butter, about 6-8 minutes
    3. In a large bowl, whisk eggs, half and half, and nutmeg
    4. Sprinkle pie crust with cheese and top with asparagus-onion mix
    5. Top with egg mixture, and bake 50-60 minutes
